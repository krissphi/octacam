About OctaCam Studio

OctaCam is a high-performance, browser-based webcam utility designed for content creators, live streamers, remote workers, and video enthusiasts. It provides instant camera controls, real-time stop-motion FPS adjustments (ranging from 1 FPS to 60 FPS), professional color grading presets, and broadcast-ready audio enhancement.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • Real-Time Chroma Key Greenscreen & Virtual Background Replacement: Supports green screen (#00ff00), blue screen (#0000ff), and custom key colors with adjustable tolerance and edge smoothness. Includes 4 zero-latency built-in virtual background presets (Studio, Cozy Loft, Neon Cyber, Office) and custom background image uploads.
  • Adobe Photoshop & Lightroom 3D LUT Support: Parse and apply custom 3D LUT .cube files with automatic localStorage persistence and quota protection.
  • Custom User Preset Saver: Save and manage custom overlay combinations, brightness, contrast, saturation, and pixelation adjustments into reusable grid preset tiles.
  • Audio Sidetone & Vocal Enhancement: Zero-latency headphone microphone monitoring with adjustable gain and 80Hz high-pass filter + 2.8kHz vocal clarity boost.
  • Camera Controls: Instant unmirrored / mirrored camera feed toggle, custom aspect ratios (16:9, 4:3, 1:1, 9:16), and instant hotkeys.
  • Floating Snapshot Gallery & Recording: Take high-resolution PNG snapshots to a floating preview drawer or record WebM video files with 1-second corruption protection.
